Rit Incoming Student's Hackathon -- august 21 & 22

    What is it?

  • A hackathon is an event where you can create anything with a group of up to 4 people. From a revolutionary Android app to a simple game, anything is permitted. This is a great opportunity for all incoming freshmen and transfer students to get to know each other and have fun at the same time.
